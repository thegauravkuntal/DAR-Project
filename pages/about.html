About Radiant Chemtech

Radiant Chemtech Pvt. Ltd is operation since year 2002 in collaboration with Wacom manufacturing co ltd. Japan for making the silicone molded products. It is ISO-9001:2015 and ISO-14001:2015 certified company. It is a part of DAR group which is well diversified into different business verticals since Year 2002. The details of the DAR group business verticals briefed on the following pages. Pioneer in making the Feature mobile phone keypads in India. Tie up with Designhousein China for making design and tools for silicone moulding Fully automatic paint line for painting the silicone products. EMS service Very high speed SMT dual line [ Panasonic] fully capable of making the most complicated Electronics products. Specially installed to manufacture Smart Phone PCB. Current products being manufactured are Smart LED TV PCBA, STB PCBA, Energy Meter PCBA, WIFI Router PCBA, PayTm Sound box PCBA, CCTV Camera PCBA, Railway control card etc.
