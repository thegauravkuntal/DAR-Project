Our Japanese Collaborators

Radiant Chemtech Pvt. Ltd is an Indian entity and was formed in technical collaboration with Wacom manufacturing company, Japan in 2002.
Wacom Manufacturing company has an experience of 30+ years in manufacturing of silicone molded products in Japan.
The technology has been passed on to Radiant Chemtech Pvt. Ltd.
They are our technology partners and advising us on latest technology and manufacturing practices in the field of Silicone rubber components.
